# Creator Blueprint

## Departments

| Department | Owns | First metric |
|---|---|---|
| Strategy | niche, offer, positioning | clear promise |
| Content | ideas, scripts, drafts | publishable backlog |
| Design | thumbnails, visuals, brand system | recognizable style |
| Distribution | platform packaging | reach per post |
| Ops | calendar, repurposing, lessons | cycle consistency |

## Intake

- Creator promise and audience.
- Primary platform.
- Content constraints: voice, topics, boundaries.
- Monetization or community goal.
- Approval rules for public posting.

## First Epics

1. Positioning one-pager.
2. Ten idea backlog with hooks.
3. One production template.
4. Weekly review ritual.

## Gates

- No public post without approval until the founder delegates the channel.
- Lessons separate audience signal from creator preference.
