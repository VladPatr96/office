# Agency Blueprint

## Departments

| Department | Owns | First metric |
|---|---|---|
| Sales | ICP, offers, pipeline | qualified conversations |
| Delivery | client work, scope, acceptance | on-time verified delivery |
| Account | client comms, expectations | client response time |
| Ops | templates, margins, staffing | delivery margin |
| QA | client-ready verification | rework rate |

## Intake

- Target client and urgent problem.
- Offer shape and price range.
- Delivery artifact.
- Approval rules for outbound messages.
- Margin and time constraints.

## First Epics

1. Offer page or brief.
2. Delivery checklist and scope boundaries.
3. First outreach package, approval-gated.
4. Retrospective template for client work.

## Gates

- Client-facing messages require approval until explicitly delegated.
- Every delivery has acceptance criteria before work starts.
